Privacy Policy

Introduction

UNDERHEAT Studio respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (username, password)
  • Preferences saved in your browser (themes, UI settings)
  • Optional submissions you provide through forms
  • Basic analytics (non‑personal usage data)

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide access to site features
  • Save your customization settings
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Maintain account security

Local Storage

Many settings (theme, UI scale, background, debug logs) are stored locally in your browser. This information never leaves your device unless you choose to submit it.

Third‑Party Services

Some features (such as Webamp skins or external links) may load assets from third‑party sources. These services may have their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • You may delete your account by contacting an administrator.
  • You may clear all local settings using the Reset options in the Settings page.
  • You may choose not to create an account and browse public content anonymously.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please reach out through the official UNDERHEAT Studio channels.
