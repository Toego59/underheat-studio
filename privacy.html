We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the site includes:

Personal Data: Username and email address when you register for an account.

Preference Data: Theme settings and color customization choices you select.

Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the site to:

4. Two-Factor Authentication

To protect your account, we require two-factor authentication. This involves sending a verification code to your registered email address. Please note that:

• Codes expire after 15 minutes
• You must enter the code to complete login
• Lost codes cannot be recovered; register a new device
• Keep your email secure to protect your account

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.
